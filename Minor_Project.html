ANUGOONJ 2020

Anugoonj is an Annual Cultural Festival of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. In this programme all the departments of University and all the affiliated colleges of University participate. This is a wonderful platform for the students to show their innate capabilities other than academic programme. Anugoonj held in two parts (a) preliminary round (b) Finals. In the Anugoonj preliminary round colleges are divided according to zones. From each zone the winners performs at the GGSIP University. Institute of Vocational studies participates every time in this event and reaches up to the competition at University level. Event Address: Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University at Dwarka Sector 16c, Delhi - 110078 Around 40 different programmes like of bands, street play, folk dance, DJ session, choreography, musical play, classical dance, Mr and Mrs Anugoonj, talent show, nach baliye, mono acting etc. are being organised during all three days of this cultural festival.
